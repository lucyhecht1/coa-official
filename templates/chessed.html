NATAL

✦ ✦ ✦ ✦

NATAL is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing psychological and emotional care for Israeli civilians and soldiers who have experienced trauma due to war and acts of terror.

Since its founding in 1998, NATAL has impacted over 500,000 individuals, offering crisis intervention, therapy, and community resilience programs.

In response to October 7th, NATAL has has deepened its commitment to emotional healing and resilience by expanding its services. Efforts include:

  • Tripling the number of volunteers on their Helpline
  • Sending mental health professionals to assist displaced communities
  • Supporting thousands of soldiers transitioning back to civilian life

With 64,000 calls fielded since the war began and 2,300 patients treated weekly, NATAL continues to be a beacon of healing and resilience.
